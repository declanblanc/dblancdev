Reclaiming Your Time

Mindful & intentional living in the age of distraction

~10 minutes
Mindfulness · Technology · Intentional living
Solo episode

Overview

In this episode, we dig into what it really means to live mindfully and intentionally — and why so many of us are unknowingly surrendering our time to platforms built to hold our attention. We explore the structural forces behind our screen habits, draw on philosophy and self-reflection exercises, and offer practical first steps to start taking your time back.
